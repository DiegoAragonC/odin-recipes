Tortilla de Patatas

Description

Tortilla de patatas o tortilla espa√±ola is an egg an potato dish
that's cooked in a skillet and served as a tapa, a main dish with a baguette
and green salad, or even enjoyed as a snack.

Ingredients

  • Olive oil
  • Yukon Gold potatoes
  • Onion
  • Eggs
  • Crushed sea salt

Preparation

  1. Add olive oil to a 10 or 12 inch skillet over medium heat.
  2. Add sliced potato and onion to the pan; they should be mostly covered with olive oil.
  3. Season with 1 1/2 teaspoon sea salt.
  4. Cook on medium high heat for 8-12 minutes, turning occasionally, until potatoes are just fork tender.
  5. Drain potatoes, reserving oil for later use. Allow them to cool for a few minutes.
  6. Crack the eggs into a bowl and season with about 1/2 teaspoon of salt.
    Beat the eggs together and pour over the cooked potatoes and onion and toss to coat.
  7. Add a little bit of oil to the skillet. Pour potato mixture into it and cook on high heat for 1 minute.
    Reduce heat to medium and cook for about 5 minutes.
  8. Flip the omelette with a plate.
  9. Cook until a knife or toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean.
